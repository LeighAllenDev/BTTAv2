Who We Are

We are a local business in the Brighton area who's number 1 passion is Retro Gaming.

We have based our Arcade around a traditional American Video Gaming arcade from the early 70's with a few moddern twists.

What We Offer

In our establishment we offer an extensive range of retro arcade games which are pay to play, or free with our premium service.

As well as a wide selection of Retro Games and Arcade machines, we have cafe to cater for adults and children, with vegan options.
